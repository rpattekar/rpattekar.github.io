Ria's Travel

I love to travel and have already gotten the opportunity to visit a bunch of amazing places! Here is a run-down of some of my favorite places and the things I enjoyed most from those places:

Domestic Travel (In the U.S.)

New York, New York
- Strolling around Central Park
- Shopping in SOHO
- Eating pasta at La Pecora Bianca in Bryant Park
San Luis Obispo, California
- Drinking an iced latte at Scout Coffee
- Shopping around the lively downtown area
- Eating super good pizza at Woodstock's Pizza with my family
Las Vegas, Nevada
- Staying at a fancy casino hotel
- Exploring the different city-themed casinos like Caesar's Palace and The Venetian
- Seeing the fountains in front of the Bellagio at night

International Travel

Paris, France
- Going to many amazing coffee shops with great outdoor ambiance
- Seeing the Eiffel Tower in person
- Shopping on the Champs Elysees
Saint Petersberg, Russia
- Going on a beautiful boat tour on the Neva River
- Trying new foods that I've never tried before
- Seeing the Peterhof Palace and learning the history behind it
Bangkok, Thailand
- Snorkeling with my cousin in super clear water
- Watching a dancing/acrobatics show with my family
- Trying amazing Thai street food like chicken satay
Tokyo, Japan
- Seeing the Shibuya crossing happening in real time
- Eating yummy matcha ice cream and great sushi
- Walking through an outdoor forest temple and learning about Japanese culture
